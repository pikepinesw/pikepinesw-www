About Us

Pike & Pine Software is an independent software company focused on building useful, well-crafted tools for everyday problems.

Our Name

Pike and pine — a fish and a tree. Two things that thrive in the same cold, clear waters of the Pacific Northwest. We believe great software should feel the same way: natural, purposeful, and built for the long haul.

Our Approach

We build small, focused products that do one thing well. We don't chase trends or add features for the sake of it. Every decision starts with the question: does this make the product genuinely better for the people using it?

Where We Stand

We're a small, independent company. No outside investors, no growth-at-all-costs mentality. We build software we'd want to use ourselves, and we stand behind everything we ship.
